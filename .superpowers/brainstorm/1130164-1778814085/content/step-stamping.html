Fretboard's job
Just answers "what note?". Click a cell → stamp at cursor. Highlighted column shows where the next stamp lands.
Timeline's job
Owns rhythm and edits. Drag bar ends to resize. Click empty space to insert a rest. Click a note to open the articulation inspector.
Step length + cursor
Picker controls how long each stamp is and how far the cursor advances. Hold ⇧ to stack notes at the same cursor position (chord).
